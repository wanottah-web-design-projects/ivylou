Inspirations


Here are some of the people or pages who have inspired me and encouraged me to do this thing.

Clicking on the links will open the pages in a new tab/window, so feel free to wander off and have a look around. Come back though! There's more good stuff.

Annie's Eats - The recipe site that got me all caught up in the blogging world to start with.

Cake Wrecks - The hilarious site that reassures me that even my cooking mistakes aren't quite as bad as I think.

Cambridge In Colour - Photo tips, and stunning images. Have a look at the gallery and sigh.

Cherry Menlove - The wonderful inspiration to be wife, home-maker, mother, crafter, and decorator extraordinaire.

Crafty Pod - A nice little collection of tutorials, tips and tricks.

Happy Together - Family life, with some crafts thrown in.

House of Gjertsen - A truly remarkable story of courage and faith through extreme adversity. I spent four days catching up on all their posts from beginning to end. I suggest you do the same.

How About Orange - A fun obsession with orange, and the crafts involved.

Noodle Head - A little bit of everything. I like that her children are involved in everything.

One Pretty Thing - A daily round up of the best craft/d.i.y. projects on the web.

Ornamelle - A beautifully simple site, focussing mainly on calligraphy and photography.

Paper Snitch - Tutorials and exhibitions of lots of lovely crafty things.

Sewing Republic - Ideas for sewing projects, should ever you need them.

Timber Butte Homestead - I've met these guys, and they are wonderful.

Zakka Life - Fun little projects for people of all ages. My favourite is the miniature coloured pencils - cute!

There are also many people in my life who have inspired me and help me be the very best of who I am. To name but a few, in no particular order: Chris, Sarah, Maggie, Dad, Naomi, Ali, Steve, Boy, Mum, Paul, Ele, John.
