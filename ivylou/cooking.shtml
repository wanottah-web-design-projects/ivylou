My Cooking


I love to cook. In most of my free time I bake. I love both 'types' of preparing food. I tend to be more creative with my baking, and focus more on taste in my cooking. I am forever looking at recipes, and working out what I can make with what's left in the cupboards! More than anything else, I love to cook for others. I love to have people over; I enjoy the company, I enjoy the challenge, and I enjoy the compliments!
